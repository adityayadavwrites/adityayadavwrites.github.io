Writing

I write emotionally driven stories with a romantic core, blending intimacy, longing, and character-first storytelling across contemporary and fantasy romance.

Completed Manuscripts

  • In The Booth
    A contemporary romance set in the world of audiobook narration, exploring voice, vulnerability, and unexpected intimacy.
  • Sparkbound
    An adult fantasy romance about power, devotion, and desire, where magic feeds on emotion and obsession becomes a language of its own.
  • Let's Escape, Babe
    A light contemporary romance set during a Goa retreat, centred on found family, emotional closeness, and unexpected attention.
  • Off-Pitch Pyar
    A Gen-Z queer contemporary romance set in Indian cricket, blending heated rivalry, forbidden attraction, found family, and internet chaos that refuses to stay off the pitch.
