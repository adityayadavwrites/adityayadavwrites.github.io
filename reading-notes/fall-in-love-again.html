Books That Made Me Fall in Love with Love

Some books don’t just tell love stories — they remind you why believing in love is worth it.

• Beach lovers — Emily Henry

Why it hit: the tenderness, the emotional honesty, and the way it let love be gentle without being boring.

• Heartstopper — Alice Oseman

Why it hit: longing done right, quiet devotion, and characters who felt deeply human.

• Lightning in a Shot Glass — Deepanjana Pal

Why it hit: A brief, beautiful ache — the kind of love story that lingers long after the last page.
