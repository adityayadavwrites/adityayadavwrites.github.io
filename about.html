About Me

I’m Aditya Yadav, a writer drawn to emotionally grounded stories with warmth, humour, and the occasional brush of magic.

I write contemporary and commercial rom-com–leaning fiction, often exploring longing, connection, and the quiet moments where people realise they’re already in love.

My work balances softness with emotional tension, blending heartfelt character dynamics with stories that feel comforting, hopeful, and quietly immersive.

Outside of writing, I'm a medical student.
